# Initial Concept

Pausalci - A comprehensive web application designed specifically for Serbian "pausalci" (flat-tax entrepreneurs) to manage their business operations, track income, calculate taxes, create invoices, and monitor payment obligations. The system provides a full Serbian-language interface with features for dashboard overview of annual income and tax limits, client management (CRUD operations), invoice management with PDF generation, income tracking against pausalac limits, and automated tax & contribution calculations for monthly obligations (tax, pension, health).

# Product Guide

## Product Vision

Pausalci is a comprehensive business management platform designed specifically for Serbian flat-tax entrepreneurs (pausalci). Our mission is to simplify business operations, ensure tax compliance, and provide affordable financial management tools that enable small entrepreneurs to focus on growing their businesses rather than administrative overhead.

## Target Audience

### Primary Users
- **Small Serbian Entrepreneurs (Pausalci)**: Individual business owners operating under the flat-tax regime who need simple, compliant invoicing and tax tracking without requiring extensive accounting knowledge
- Typically service providers, consultants, freelancers, and small-scale traders
- Users who value simplicity, automation, and compliance over complex accounting features
- Price-conscious entrepreneurs seeking affordable solutions

### User Pain Points
- Complex tax regulations and frequent changes in Serbian tax law
- Manual calculation of pausalni limits and obligations
- Time-consuming invoice creation and tracking
- Risk of exceeding annual income limits
- Lack of affordable, localized solutions
- Compliance with new e-invoice requirements

## Core Value Proposition

Pausalci offers Serbian flat-tax entrepreneurs an all-in-one solution that:
- **Automates Compliance**: Automatic calculation and tracking of pausalni tax limits and obligations specific to Serbian regulations
- **Ensures E-Invoice Readiness**: Built-in compliance with Serbian e-invoice standards (eFaktura/SEF) with automatic submission capabilities
- **Prevents Limit Breaches**: Real-time income tracking with proactive warnings when approaching annual pausalni limits
- **Reduces Costs**: 50% more affordable than competing solutions while providing comprehensive features
